Tree Estimating Definitions

Normal Rigging

Standard controlled lowering using ropes.

Technical Rigging

Complex rigging, negative blocking, tight targets, limited tie-ins.

Near Structure

Tree is near structures but manageable.

High-Risk Structure

Tree directly over structure with high consequence.

Dead / Brittle

Weak or compromised wood.

Poor Drop Zone

Limited landing space.

Bad Access

Difficult equipment or drag distance.

Steep Terrain

Slope makes work slower or harder.

Heavy Cleanup

Lots of debris or brush.

XL Logs

Large heavy wood handling.
