Difficulty Definitions

Normal Rigging

Standard controlled lowering with ropes. Routine rope work.

Technical Rigging

Complex rigging, negative blocking, limited tie-ins, heavy pieces over targets, or slower high-risk rope work.

Near Structure/Fence

Tree is near a house, fence, shed, driveway, or other feature, but risk is manageable.

High-Risk Structure/Fence

Tree or major limbs are directly over a structure, or a mistake would likely cause expensive damage.

Dead/Brittle

Compromised wood, dead limbs, brittle canopy, hollow areas, or unsafe tie-in/rigging concerns.

Poor Drop Zone

Limited landing area, obstacles below, tight yard, landscaping, fences, or other restrictions that slow production.

Bad Access / Long Drag

Difficult machine access, long hand-carry/drag distance, tight gate, or limited staging area.

Steep Terrain

Sloped or unstable terrain that slows cleanup, machine work, climbing, dragging, or hauling.

Heavy Cleanup / Wood Volume

Large amount of brush, limbs, debris, or cleanup time beyond a normal removal.

XL Logs / Oversized Wood

Large diameter wood, usually over 32 inches, requiring extra machine time, cutting, loading, or handling.
